ABOUT

Hi there! ^1000 I’m Blair. ^2000
You may be wondering how I ended up as a full stack developer…

I studied Art History in undergrad, and eventually became a graphic designer and social media professional in the fashion industry in DTLA. I was always interested in coding, ever since I started tinkering around with HTML and CSS back in the Myspace days. I took some intro Computer Science classes in college, and early last year I started seriously teaching myself how to build websites. I focused mostly on HTML, CSS, JS/jQuery and responsive design techniques, with a little bit of PHP as well.

Eventually, I felt that I would benefit from a more structured learning environment, and I was ready to quit my job and take the leap into developing! I settled on General Assembly’s 12 week long Web Development Immersive bootcamp, which I completed in December 2016. We learned web dev basics, Ruby on Rails, as well as the full MEAN stack. For my last project I taught myself the Ionic mobile framework, and built a mobile app called FluffMates.

Coding combines my loves of good design, building things, being creative, solving problems, and drinking way too much coffee in ways I never imagined! I can’t wait to find my home on a dev team near you. I’m also available for freelance or contract work remotely, or in the LA area. I love DTLA and would be especially stoked to find a position in my neighborhood.
